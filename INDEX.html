About

Established in 1997, Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering helps students find the right career by offering undergraduate degrees
in six different fields of engineering, and post graduate degrees in three different fields. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological
University (VTU), Belgaum, approved by AICTE, New Delhi and recognized by the Govt. of Karnataka
